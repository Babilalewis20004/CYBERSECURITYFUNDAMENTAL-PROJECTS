Hobbies

Drawing

Memory: I used to spend hours drawing cartoon characters. It was a wonderful way to express my creativity.

Memory: I remember winning an art competition in school with a drawing of a nature scene. It made me so proud!

Reading

Memory: I loved reading adventure books. I would get lost in the stories for hours on end.

Memory: My parents bought me a collection of classic novels for my birthday, which I still cherish today.

Toys

Action Figures

Memory: My action figures were my favorite toys. I created epic battles and adventures with them.

Memory: I used to swap action figures with my friends, which made playtime even more exciting.

Building Blocks

Memory: I built everything from castles to spaceships with my building blocks. It was like having a new toy every day.

Memory: I once constructed a huge tower that almost reached the ceiling, and I felt like a little architect.
